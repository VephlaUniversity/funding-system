FAQs from DTFS

Got Questions?
We've Got Answers

Who is Eligible to Apply?
Anyone passionate about technology and ready to learn can apply. However, we prioritize those with strong motivation, clear goals, and a genuine need for financial support or mentorship.
What is the Mentorship & Transition Aid program?
Eligibility requirements include enrollment in an accredited educational institution, maintaining a minimum GPA, and demonstrating financial need. Specific programs may have additional requirements.
What Does the Funding Scheme Cover?
Our application deadlines vary by program. The main scholarship application deadline is typically March 31st each year, while specialized programs may have different deadlines throughout the year.
How Do I Apply?
Our scholarships may cover tuition fees, books, supplies, and in some cases, living expenses. The exact coverage depends on the specific scholarship program you're applying for.
What Is the Selection Process & Timeline?
You can check your application status by logging into your account on our application portal. All updates will be communicated through your registered email address.
Is the Support Fully Funded?
Yes, all scholarship recipients are automatically enrolled in our mentorship program where they are paired with industry professionals or academic mentors based on their field of study.
